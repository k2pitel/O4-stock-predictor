Stock Price Direction Prediction using Machine Learning

Bleron Gashi & Kevin Pham  |  Group 29  |  SWMAL  |  Aarhus University  |  2026
Introduction
  • Binary classification: predict next-day stock direction (↑/↓)
  • AAPL, JPM, TSLA  |  2016–2025  |  ~2,464 trading days
  • Data: Yahoo Finance + VIX index
  • Supervised learning + Prophet forecasting + trading strategy
Materials and Methods

26 features: MA (10/20/50d), RSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands, volatility (10/20d), momentum (1/5d), lagged close (1–5d), daily return, VIX, volume

7 models: Logistic Regression, Random Forest, SVM, XGBoost, Voting Ensemble, MLP, LSTM

Pipeline: 80/20 chronological split → StandardScaler → GridSearchCV (TimeSeriesSplit k=3) → Train → Evaluate

Cost fn: Binary Cross-Entropy (MLP/LSTM) | Gini impurity (trees)

Overfitting: Dropout 0.2–0.3, L2 reg, max_depth constraints

Conclusions
  • All models ~50–54% accuracy — consistent with EMH
  • TSLA most predictable; ML strategy beats Buy & Hold (+163% vs +120%)
  • JPM best risk-adjusted: Sharpe 1.54, max drawdown −9%
  • Dropout + L2 reg prevents overfitting in MLP and LSTM
